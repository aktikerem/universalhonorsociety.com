Who are we?

The Universal Honor Society (UHS) is a student-led organization founded by high school students from around the world, dedicated to raising public awareness about global issues related to polities, diplomacy, international law, and international relations.

Our Message

Beyond the typical want to contribute to wider society in an altruistic way, we want something more than simply cooperating and helping others with the resources given. We want to make sure it lasts.
